  1. Cows are herbivorous animals and feed on grasses and other vegetation.
  2. They have a lifespan of around 10-15 years.
  3. Cows produce milk as part of their natural diet.
  4. The average cow produces around 6 to 7 pounds of milk per day.
  5. Cows have a unique ability to digest cellulose, making them highly efficient at converting grass into food.
  6. They have a large heart that can beat up to 30 times per minute and can consume around 22 gallons of blood each day.
  7. The average cow has around 5,000 muscles in its body.
  8. Cows are social animals and live in groups called herds.
  9. They have a unique scent that helps them navigate and find their way back to the same grazing area.
  10. Cows can be trained to use tools such as cups, carrots, or even a hula hoop.
